Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 06 September 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how PiqTok (the “App”) handles information. The App is designed to operate without collecting, storing, or sharing any personal data from users.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

  • The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.
  • The App does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or third‑party SDKs.
  • The App does not create user accounts or use identifiers.

2. Photos Access

The App requests permission to access your Photos library solely to help you review and delete photos on your device. All processing occurs on your device. No photos or related metadata are uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

3. Third Parties

We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect data. Note that your device manufacturer (Apple) and the App Store may independently collect diagnostic or purchase information subject to their own policies.

4. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the App does not collect data, there is no personal information to manage or delete.

5. Security

Since the App does not collect or store data, there are no user records on our servers. Standard device protections apply to content stored on your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

If our practices change (for example, if we introduce optional online features), we will update this policy and the effective date. Material changes will be reflected before they take effect.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact us at cytrix@me.com.
