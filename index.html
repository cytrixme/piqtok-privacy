How can we help?

PiqTok helps you review and curate photos quickly. This page provides help and contact options.

FAQs

  • Does PiqTok upload my photos? No. All browsing and curation happen locally on your device.
  • Permissions needed? Access to your photo library to display and manage photos.
  • Having trouble? Force-close the app and reopen. Ensure Photos access is granted in Settings → Privacy → Photos.
